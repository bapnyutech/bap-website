Who We Are
VITA (Volunteer Income Tax Assistance) is a program that allows IRS-certified volunteers to provide free basic income tax return preparation for low-income individuals. Low-income individuals are those who make approximately $57,000 or less annually. We also offer services to NYU students including international students.

Disclaimers
[COVID DISCLAIMER] Due to the COVID pandemic, we will be 100% online in order to protect the safety of our volunteers. We will not be able to meet in person as many volunteers are not currently situated in New York. In order to carry out VITA, we will require clients to have video conference capabilities (e.g. a video camera) as well as an internet connection (to call and upload the necessary tax information). If you have filed with NYU VITA (must be at the same location) in the previous year, we may be able to assist you without the need for a video call if you have not changed your method of receiving your tax refund and have not moved or changed address. Our main platforms will be Zoom and Dropbox, and we can only offer limited accommodations to our clients who may have trouble accessing these platforms to protect the information of our clients, such as using email instead of Dropbox.

We will also be creating a step by step instructional page that will outline the steps needed for a successful virtual tax filing and the instructions needed to upload the necessary tax documents. Please check back soon. Thank you!
